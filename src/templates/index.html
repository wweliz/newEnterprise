Age Restrictions

In the U.S., renters age 18-20 can only rent from locations in New York or Michigan, or if they are U.S. government employees renting for authorized government travel. U.S. government employees age 18 or older will not incur a young-driver surcharge when renting for authorized government travel.

Reservations for ages 18-20 must be made by phone or in person, and cannot be made online.
